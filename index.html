Cosmic IPA

Our Cosmic IPA is a bold, hop-forward brew crafted with precision. Bursting with citrus and pine notes, it delivers a crisp, refreshing finish that elevates every sip. Perfect for those who crave adventure in every glass.

This American IPA combines modern brewing techniques with classic hop varieties, creating a vibrant, aromatic beer with a crisp finish.

Expect bright citrus, subtle pine, and a balanced malt backbone that makes every sip a journey through flavor.

Pine

Malt Warmth

A smooth caramel malt base provides balance, grounding the hops with a touch of sweetness.
